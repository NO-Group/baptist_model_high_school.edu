How to Pay

Follow these simple steps to complete your payment

1

Make Payment

Transfer or deposit the required amount to any of our bank accounts listed above.

2

Keep Your Receipt

Obtain and keep your bank teller or transfer receipt safe for verification.

3

Submit Receipt

Bring the original receipt to the school bursar's office for documentation.

4

Collect School Receipt

Receive your official school receipt which your child must present at resumption.

üìã Important Notes

  • All fees must be paid in full before the beginning of each term. Students with outstanding fees may not be allowed to resume.
  • Please use your child's full name and class as the payment reference when making bank transfers.
  • Fees are non-refundable once the term has commenced unless in exceptional circumstances approved by management.
  • Parents are encouraged to pay early to avoid rush and ensure smooth resumption for their children.
  • For any fee-related inquiries, please contact the Bursar's office during school hours or send an email to bursar@bmhsilorin.edu.ng
  • Installment payment arrangements may be available in special cases. Please contact the school administration for details.
