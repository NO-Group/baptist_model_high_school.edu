Frequently Asked Questions

What are the school's operating hours? +
The school operates Monday through Friday from 7:30 AM to 3:30 PM. The administrative office is also open during these hours for inquiries and visits.
How can I apply for admission? +
You can apply for admission by visiting our Parent Portal page and filling out the online application form. Alternatively, you can visit the school in person to obtain a physical application form.
What are the school fees? +
Our fee structure varies by class level. Please visit our Fees page for detailed information on tuition and other charges. You can also contact our Bursar's office for specific inquiries.
Can I schedule a school visit? +
Yes! We welcome prospective parents and students to visit our campus. Please call us or send an email to schedule a convenient time for your visit. We'll be happy to show you around.
What curriculum does the school follow? +
We follow the Nigerian National Curriculum approved by the Federal Ministry of Education. Our students are prepared for WAEC, NECO, and BECE examinations. Visit our Academics page for more details.
Does the school provide transportation? +
Yes, we offer an optional school bus service for students. The bus covers various routes within Ilorin and its environs. Please contact the school for route information and fees.
