Examinations

Preparing students for national and international examinations

WAEC (WASSCE)

West African Senior School Certificate Examination - The primary school leaving examination for secondary schools.

  • Intensive preparation classes
  • Past question practice
  • Mock examinations
  • 95%+ pass rate history

NECO

National Examination Council - An alternative national examination recognized across Nigeria.

  • Comprehensive coverage
  • Regular assessments
  • Individual attention
  • Excellent results track record

BECE (Junior WAEC)

Basic Education Certificate Examination for Junior Secondary School completion.

  • Foundational preparation
  • Skill assessment
  • Career guidance
  • Progression planning
