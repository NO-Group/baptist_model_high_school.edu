Mr. Adebayo Samuel Oluwaseun

Principal

Mr. Adebayo Samuel Oluwaseun has been leading Baptist Model High School with dedication and vision since 2018. With over 25 years of experience in education, he brings a wealth of knowledge and passion for academic excellence.

He holds a Master's degree in Educational Administration from the University of Ilorin and is committed to nurturing students who will become future leaders in their various fields.

"Education is not just about acquiring knowledge; it's about building character, developing skills, and preparing young minds to serve God and humanity. At BMHS, we study to serve."
